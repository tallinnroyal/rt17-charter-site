Accommodation is not included in the event price, giving everyone the freedom to choose where they prefer to stay. Tallinn offers a wide range of options through Booking.com and Airbnb, from budget to boutique — and thanks to the city’s compact size, everything is within a short taxi ride. Our recommendation: Metropol Hotel (www.metropol.ee), a trusted choice for Tablers during previous international events, conveniently located in the city center..
